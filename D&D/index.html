The Chosen’s Ascent

Party Roster for our Tales-of-Symphonia–style One Shot

Find Your Character

Choose your name from the list to see your class, role, and details.

Aria

The Chosen · Level 4 Cleric

Race: Human (or Aasimar) Subclass: Light Domain Primary Role: Healer / Support / Radiant Nuker

Marked by prophecy and burdened with the task of restoring mana to the world, Aria is the official Chosen. Their divine magic manifests as blinding radiance and protective wards. After each major trial, that same power strains their body and mind, sometimes costing them pieces of their senses.

Key Features

  • Divine Pulse: Once per short rest, heal an ally, blast an enemy, or shield a friend.
  • Sense Strain: After each Act, roll to see if another sense is weakened.
  • Party Anchor: Many story beats will revolve around you. Big RP energy.

Kael

Blade of Sylvarant · Level 4 Fighter

Race: Human Subclass: Battle Master (recommended) Primary Role: Melee Damage / Control

A disciplined swordsman trained to protect the Chosen at any cost. Kael channels classic JRPG “sword hero” energy—darting into the fray, carving through Desians, and using maneuvers to protect squishier allies.

Key Features

  • Extra Attack: Two swings per turn. Very anime.
  • Battle Maneuvers: Trip, push, or parry enemies.
  • Frontline Guardian: Stand between threats and the Chosen.

Lira

Shadow of the Human Ranches · Level 4 Rogue

Race: Half-Elf Subclass: Assassin or Arcane Trickster Primary Role: Burst Damage / Skill Monkey

Lira escaped a Desian facility years ago and has no love for angels or overseers. She believes the Chosen’s journey hides a darker truth and intends to uncover it with a dagger in one hand and a lockpick in the other.

Key Features

  • Sneak Attack: Bonus damage when you have advantage or an ally nearby.
  • Stealth & Tools: Great at scouting ahead and disarming traps.
  • Social Edge: Perfect for shady deals and interrogations.

Thorne

Oathbound Protector · Level 4 Paladin

Race: Dwarf or Human Subclass: Oath of Devotion or Ancients Primary Role: Tank / Burst Smite

Sworn to guard the Chosen, Thorne sees this pilgrimage as both a holy duty and last chance to redeem the fading world. They stand in the front line, shield raised, daring Desians and false angels alike to try their worst.

Key Features

  • Divine Smite: Dump spell slots for radiant nova damage.
  • Aura & Aids: Support nearby allies with buffs and heals.
  • Anchor: Great at soaking hits meant for allies.

Nyx

Mana Scholar · Level 4 Wizard

Race: Elf Subclass: Evocation or Divination Primary Role: Area Damage / Utility

Nyx has spent years researching why mana is vanishing from the world. Traveling with the Chosen is the best (and most dangerous) way to test their theories about seals, angels, and parallel worlds.

Key Features

  • Big Spells: Crowd-control and area damage.
  • Ritual Casting: Free out-of-combat utility magic.
  • Glass Cannon: Squishy, keep them protected.

Bram

Mana-Hardened Raider · Level 4 Barbarian

Race: Half-Orc or Goliath Subclass: Berserker or Totem Warrior Primary Role: Melee Bruiser

Bram survived in the wilderness long after mana-scarred beasts made travel nearly impossible. He fights not for prophecy, but for his friends—and to see Desian facilities burn.

Key Features

  • Rage: Bonus damage and resistance to physical hits.
  • Simple Gameplan: Run at it. Hit it. Hit it again.
  • Survivable: Great for newer players.

Syl

Warden of the Borderlands · Level 4 Ranger

Race: Elf or Human Subclass: Hunter or Gloom Stalker Primary Role: Ranged DPS / Exploration

Syl has watched the forests wither as mana drains away. Tracking monsters, scouting enemy bases, and striking from afar are second nature—and now all of that skill is turned toward keeping the Chosen alive.

Key Features

  • Reliable Ranged Damage: Bow attacks each round.
  • Exploration Tools: Great at travel, tracking, and survival.
  • Flexible: Works in melee or at range.

Mira

Voice of the People · Level 4 Bard

Race: Half-Elf Subclass: College of Valor or Lore Primary Role: Buffs / Healing / Social

Mira travels with the Chosen not because of prophecy, but to make sure their story is told the right way. With inspiring words, cutting remarks, and a bit of healing magic, she keeps hope alive even when everything else is falling apart.

Key Features

  • Bardic Inspiration: Boost allies’ rolls at clutch moments.
  • Support Spells: Healing, buffs, debuffs.
  • Social Powerhouse: Perfect for roleplay scenes.
